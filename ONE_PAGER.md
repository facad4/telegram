# Telegram Updates Dashboard

A real-time, auto-scrolling dashboard that aggregates posts from your Telegram channels into a single, unified feed — designed for always-on displays, team monitoring, or personal use.

---

## What It Does

Telegram Updates Dashboard pulls the latest posts from any combination of public Telegram channels and presents them in a continuously scrolling, TV-friendly interface. Think of it as a live news ticker for your Telegram world.

## Key Features

**Unified Feed**
Aggregate posts from dozens of channels into one chronological stream. Public channels are scraped directly from Telegram's web preview. Posts display rich content including text, images, video thumbnails, and link previews.

**Auto-Scrolling Display**
The dashboard scrolls automatically at a configurable speed, loops back to the top, and pauses intelligently on user interaction. Perfect for wall-mounted screens, dashboards, or ambient monitoring. Users can stop, resume, or manually sync at any time.

**Channel Discovery**
Built-in search lets users find and add public channels with autocomplete. 

**Save for Later**
Bookmark any post to a personal saved collection. Saved posts persist across sessions and are accessible from a dedicated view.

**Share Anywhere**
Every post has a share button offering WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, and clipboard copy.

**Light & Dark Themes**
A clean light theme (default) inspired by modern reading interfaces, plus a dark theme suited for low-light environments and display screens.

**Installable PWA**
Install the app to your home screen on iOS, Android, or desktop for a full-screen, app-like experience with no browser chrome.

**Channel Filtering**
A scrollable filter bar in the header lets you focus on a specific channel or view all channels at once.

## Who It's For
- **Individuals** who want a single dashboard instead of scrolling through Telegram
- **News & media teams** monitoring multiple Telegram sources
- **Researchers** tracking channels across topics
- **Communities** displaying a shared feed on a screen
